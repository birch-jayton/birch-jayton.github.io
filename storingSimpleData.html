Storing and Retrieving Simple Data

❯

Storing and receiving simple data was explored in the last lesson. But lets look at some more details of storing and getting data. There are different ways to approach the syntax. In our example of remembering names we wrote "localStorage.firstName = firstName;"

It can also be written as "localStorage.setElement("firstName", firstName);" Notice the difference on lines 5 and 6 between the two snippets.
